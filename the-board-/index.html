The Board

The school is overseen and governed by a school board which provides accountability and vision for the school.  Presently AHLC has representatives (including a non-voting staff representative) who meet monthly and, as required, for special events and sub- committees.  

The Board of AHLC consists of six members. The following members can be contacted at the email address below:

Lesley Richardson as our Board Chair.

Chris Rupke is our Volunteer C.E.O. and board member.   He joined AHLC in November 2015.

Please contact Chris Rupke or Lesley Richardson by email (see below) for any inquiries about the governance of African Hope Learning Centre.
